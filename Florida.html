Florida

Florida is the southeasternmost U.S. state, with the Atlantic on one side and the Gulf of Mexico on the other. It has hundreds of beaches in the state and its known for their cities and their famous theme parks, The capital of Florida is Tallahassee.

Five Facts About Florida

1. Florida is the most hurricane-prone state in the U.S.

2. Native americans lived there for at least 12,000 years before the europenas came

3. Nicknamed the sunshine state

4. Its the flattest state in the USA

5. Florida has more golf courses than any other state in the U.S.
