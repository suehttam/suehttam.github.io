Virginia Beach

Virginia Beach is a coastal city in southeastern Virginia, lies where the Chesapeake Bay meets the Atlantic Ocean. Its a three mile boardwalk and it streches along the beach front.

Five facts about Virginia Beach

1.The Virginia Beach boardwalk was originially built in 1888

2.Virginia Beach is the largest city in Virginia

3.Approximately 6 million people visit Virginia Beach each year on vacation

4.Virginia Beach's climate is considered to be humid subtropical.

5.Virginia Beach is very large geographically with 497.3 square miles in size and space.
